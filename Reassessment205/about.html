Spreading the love and joy of keeping beetles

Since a young age, my obessession in keeping beetles began when i first entered a small shop with the largest beetle i saw on display, i still remembered me leaving with a atlas beetle and a big ol smile on my face alongside my parents. In order to give out such joy to others around, this site was created in order to create smiles around the beetle and share the joy of beetle keeping worldwide
